About Me

I am enthusiastic about design, from macro to micro scale.

As a user experience designer, it's my mission to create an intuitive and delightful user experience in every step of the user’s journey. By constantly challenging myself in my design work, I keep up-to-date with new skills and technologies in interface design. I have excellent design aesthetics and efficient time management skills. I enjoy finding creative solutions through technology and design while maintaining superb quality.

I am a problem solver who enjoys new challenges. I enjoy designing products that deliver an accessible and useful user experience. I am always open to learn and grow.

UX - I get under the hood of products and explore how to communicate/design the best it can be, for those using it and beyond. Using a whole range of tools and methods, I make the output of these discoveries, turn into something tangible and keep your users engaged with frictionless experiences.

UI - I create tangible interfaces. I work alongside to translate flows, prototypes, and content into visually appealing and emotional user-centric designs. I ensure the layout, styling and execution of different visual assets are consistent with the brand.
